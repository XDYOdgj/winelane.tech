DreamLine Unidoor Toulon Frameless Hinged Shower Door

Transform your bathroom with this elegant and modern frameless shower door. Designed to blend style with practicality, it offers a clean, open look that enhances any bathroom decor. The frameless construction is not only visually striking but also allows for a more seamless and spacious feel.

Crafted from premium 3/8 in tempered glass, this door ensures durability and safety. The ClearMax coating simplifies cleaning by preventing water spots and soap scum buildup, making it a great choice for everyday use. Its sleek design is complemented by the Satin Black hardware finish, adding a refined touch to contemporary bathrooms.

Whether you're renovating or building new, this shower door is ideal for creating a modern, spa-like environment.
