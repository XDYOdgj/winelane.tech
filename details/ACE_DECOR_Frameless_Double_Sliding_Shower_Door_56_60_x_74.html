ACE DECOR Frameless Double Sliding Shower Door

This sleek and modern frameless double sliding shower door is designed to elevate your bathroom's appeal while offering practical benefits. Perfect for any contemporary bathroom, this door brings a minimalist aesthetic that seamlessly blends with modern interiors.

Equipped with 5/16" (8mm) thick clear tempered glass, the door is built for durability and safety. The nano waterproof coating helps keep the glass clean and free from water spots, meaning less maintenance and more time enjoying your bathroom.
