Fostoria OCH2 Infrared Heater

Efficient and Safe Heating for Any Space

The Fostoria OCH2 infrared heater is designed for efficient, non-glare warmth using advanced dual quartz tubes and premium gold anodized aluminum reflectors. This combination ensures even and consistent heat distribution, making it perfect for heating people or objects in residential, commercial, or industrial settings. With its quiet operation and minimal maintenance requirements, this heater is ideal for use in areas where noise and upkeep are concerns.

Thanks to its durable construction and corrosion-resistant components, the OCH2 is built to withstand harsh or damp environments. Whether it's a garage, patio, loading dock, or bus shelter, this heater delivers exceptional performance and longevity. Its medium-wave infrared technology provides heat through convection, conduction, and radiation, offering a natural and comfortable warmth without the need for a fan or moving parts.

The lightweight design of this electric heater ensures easy installation and flexible use. The mounting brackets allow a 45-degree tilt, enabling targeted heating for specific zones. Available for indoor or outdoor spot heating, this heater is ETL listed for safe use in the USA and Canada, giving you peace of mind with every use.

  • Lightweight and quiet operation for quick warmth
  • Strong steel housing with durable, textured brown finish
  • Mounting brackets allow 45-degree tilt for targeted heating
