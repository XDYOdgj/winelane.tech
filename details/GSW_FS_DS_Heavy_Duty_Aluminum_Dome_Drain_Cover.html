GSW FS-DS Drain Cover - Durable and Reliable for All Your Drain Needs

Designed for both residential and commercial use, the GSW FS-DS drain cover is built to last with thick, heavy-duty aluminum construction. This robust material ensures long-term strength, even in high-traffic or wet environments, making it a dependable choice for kitchens, bathrooms, and food service areas.

Key Benefits

  • Heavy-duty aluminum construction for unmatched durability and strength
  • Anti-clogging design with a convex dome shape that prevents debris buildup
  • Encourages smooth water flow, reducing the risk of clogs and maintaining cleanliness
  • Easy to install and remove without the need for screws or additional fasteners

The GSW FS-DS is an excellent choice for those who want a leak-proof drain cover that blends well with modern aesthetics. Its smooth edges provide a safe and comfortable experience for users, while its weighted design ensures it stays securely in place without any additional support.

Whether you're in a home kitchen or a busy restaurant, the FS-DS offers a reliable and efficient solution that simplifies your plumbing maintenance and keeps your spaces clean and dry.
