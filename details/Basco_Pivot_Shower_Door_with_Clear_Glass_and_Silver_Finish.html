Basco Pivot Shower Door

Elevate your bathroom with a stunning shower enclosure that blends style and functionality. This modern pivot door is designed to create a spa-like ambiance while maintaining a sleek, open feel.

Sleek and Durable Glass

The Basco Pivot Shower Door is crafted from thick, 0.188 inch clear glass that delivers both strength and a refined appearance. Its clarity enhances the sense of space, while the silver finish adds a touch of sophistication to your bathroom decor.

Perfect Fit for Your Space

With an overall height of 67 inches and a walk-through width of 29.81 inches, this door is engineered to fit most standard shower dimensions. The 1.81 inch bottom threshold is specially designed to prevent water leakage and keep your shower area dry and clean.

Easy Installation and Versatile Use

This universal shower door is ideal for both fiberglass and tile shower bases. It’s designed to swing out only, ensuring a seamless, trouble-free operation. Plus, the pivot jamb allows for simple out-of-square adjustments, making it adaptable to a variety of bathroom layouts and installations.
