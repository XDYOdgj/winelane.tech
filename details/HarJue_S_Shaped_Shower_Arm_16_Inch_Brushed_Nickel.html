The HarJue S Shaped Shower Arm

Transform your shower into a spa-like retreat with the HarJue S Shaped Shower Arm. Crafted from premium SUS304 stainless steel, this arm offers unmatched durability and rust resistance, ensuring it lasts for years without losing its sleek form. The Z-S shape design extends the shower head by 16 inches and raises it by 8.4 inches, perfect for taller users who want better water flow and a more comfortable height.

With its stylish brushed nickel finish, the HarJue S Shaped Shower Arm adds a touch of modern elegance to any bathroom, regardless of your decor style. Its universal 1/2 NPT threads make it compatible with most standard shower heads, offering a convenient and versatile upgrade. Designed for ease of use, this product requires no professional plumber, making it an ideal choice for DIYers who want to improve their bathroom quickly and affordably.
