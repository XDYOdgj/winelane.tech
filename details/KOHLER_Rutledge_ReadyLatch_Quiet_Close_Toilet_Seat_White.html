Kohler Rutledge ReadyLatch Quiet Close Toilet Seat

The Kohler Rutledge ReadyLatch Quiet Close Toilet Seat is crafted to bring comfort and convenience to your daily bathroom routine. Its innovative quiet-close mechanism ensures a smooth, noiseless shut, making it perfect for households that prioritize peace and quiet. The unique design allows for easy installation using the included hardware from above the bowl, leaving no need to reach underneath for setup.

With ReadyLatch hinges, this toilet seat offers a secure lock and effortless unlock function. This ensures reliable hold while making cleaning and adjustments simple and hassle-free. The durable white finish and Grip-Tight bumpers provide a firm and stable fit, keeping the seat securely in place with no wobbling or slippage.

Whether you have elderly family members, young children, or just prefer a more user-friendly design, this toilet seat enhances accessibility. Its compact, round-front style blends effortlessly with most bathroom decors, offering both practicality and a modern aesthetic. The slow-close feature also helps reduce wear and tear, adding long-term value to your bathroom fixtures.
