CRL Satin Anodized Aluminum Deep U-Channel for 3/8" Thick Glass

Looking for a reliable and stylish solution for your frameless shower door? This deep U-channel is the perfect choice. It's crafted from durable satin anodized aluminum, designed for effortless and secure installation of 3/8" thick monolithic tempered glass.

The deep pocket design ensures a tight fit, preventing glass from shifting and offering a solid base for fixed panels. Combined with wet glazing using clear silicone, it provides a seamless, water-resistant bond that enhances both safety and the overall aesthetic of your bathroom.

Whether you're upgrading an existing shower or building a new one, this U-channel is ideal for both residential and commercial use. Comes with end caps to give a clean, finished look and is available in popular stock lengths like 95" and 144", making it perfect for a wide range of applications.

Choose this product for its strength, precision, and design versatility. It's the key to achieving a modern, frameless shower experience that lasts for years.
